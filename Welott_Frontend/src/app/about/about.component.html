Welcome

Since 1957, W.E. LOTT has established a solid reputation as a trusted provider of superior cast, forged, fabricated and machined components to industrial OEM manufacturers across a multitude of industries. With offices in both the United States and Asia, we are uniquely able to offer our customers the cost advantages of international sourcing with the added security of a domestic supplier. Our best practices of tightly controlled manufacturing processes, quality assurance programs, motivated workforce, and a continuous improvement culture ensure customers that each transaction with W.E. LOTT will be met with the same outcome of quality and care, every time. In addition, our global inventory management coupled with local warehousing allows customers immediate access to offshore components without carrying the inventory.

If you are looking to maximize your products’ ability to compete in today’s rapidly changing global market, we are the company to call. With almost 60 years of industry experience, we provide the best total cost solution for your cast, forged, fabricated and machined parts. Call us today for a free quote!
